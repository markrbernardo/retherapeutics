Bio

Mark has had the opportunity to work with UC Berkeley's Men's Swim and Basketball teams as a sports massage therapist for 2 years. There, he worked with Olympians such as: Nathan Adrian, Anthony Ervin, Tom Shields, and many others. After his time at Cal, he became a performer with Disney on Ice, where he provided a massage therapy service for the touring unit for 5 years.


With his background in nutrition and expertise in sports massage therapy, he hopes to utilize his skills as a massage therapist to not only improve his client's physical mobility, but to also educate his clients on potential steps they can take to achieve better overall health.
